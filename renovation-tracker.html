Renovation tracker

A social design project focused on improving how heritage home owners navigate renovation processes.

CONTEXT

With the Estonian National Heritage Board and the Climate Ministry. Heritage renovation runs for years. Rules, steps, and paperwork sit in different places, and it is easy to lose the thread.

PROBLEM

  • Heritage home renovation is long and fragmented.
  • People lose track of where they are in the process.
  • Information is scattered across different sources.

SOLUTION

  • A simple tracking and management tool.
  • Helps users pick up where they left off.
  • Provides relevant resources and contacts at each step.

KEY IDEA

Renovation takes years — the system should remember things for you.

WHAT I DESIGNED

A lightweight tool that helps heritage home owners track their renovation progress, understand next steps, and access the right information at the right time.
