Tactile Sudoku

03

Exploring how touch, contrast, and clear structure can make puzzles easier to navigate — especially when vision isn’t the primary sense.

6 weeks (Mar–Apr 2026) · In progress

Tools: Claude, Arduino, Autodesk Fusion

Paper Prototype

Work in progress

First prototype made out of air-dry clay to test the concept.

Tactile intent

Raised borders, differently shaped pawns, and haptic feedback are intended to allow people with low vision to solve the classic puzzle of sudoku.

Pawns of different shapes and surface represent the numbers. Player relies on touch and vibration to navigate the board.
