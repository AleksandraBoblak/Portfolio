How to play

Fill every row, column, and three-by-three box with digits 1–9 without repeats. This spread keeps the puzzle as the hero; interaction will sit on top of the same grid later.

Two columns echo an editorial text flow — wide margins, clear hierarchy, nothing crowded.

Tactile intent

Raised cells, contrast, and obvious affordances are the goal for the finished piece. For now, light shadow on each square suggests depth without breaking the quiet palette of the rest of the site.

Body copy stays at the same size and rhythm as other project pages.
