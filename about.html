Hi there! I'm Aleksandra.

I'm a designer who loves focusing on the quiet, emotional details of how we experience the world. Whether I'm designing a digital interface or working with raw materials, I believe that the best work comes from a place of empathy and play.

For me, design is less about perfection and polish, and much more about human understanding.
