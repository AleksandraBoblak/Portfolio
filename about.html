Hi there! I'm Aleksandra.

I'm a designer in the making who loves to stray away from the path to try new things.

I come from linguistics and work as a translator in finance, translating everything from UI to ads.

Somewhere along the way, I developed a habit of spotting every inconsistency, typo, and slightly awkward phrase. Very useful at work. Questionable at parties.

I like working on messy systems — things that almost make sense but don’t quite. I enjoy tightening them up and turning “good enough” into something calm and clear.

I’m organized, structured, and probably the one who will make a plan so we don’t have to pull an all-nighter later.

I care about efficiency — reducing friction, cutting wait times, and treating people with respect. No tricks, no pressure — just clear, honest design.

Skills: Figma, Prototyping, Jira, ChatGPT, Cursor.
